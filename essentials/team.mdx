---

<img className="block w-full rounded-lg" src="/images/team.png" alt="Add Team Members" />

<li>From the home page, hit `G then S` to navigate to Settings.</li>
<li>Click on the `Members` tab.</li>
<li>Click `Invite` to generate invite code</li>
<li>Enter their email and select their role (`Admin` or `Member`)</li>
<li>Copy the invite url and share with the team member.</li>

<Info>We are working on adding the functionality to send email directly to recepients.</Info>
